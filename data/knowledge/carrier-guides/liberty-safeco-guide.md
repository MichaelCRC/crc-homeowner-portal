# CRC Homeowner Guide -- Liberty Mutual / Safeco

## LIBERTY MUTUAL / SAFECO

### What to Expect
Safeco is a subsidiary of Liberty Mutual. Safeco uses independent adjusters who are generally more flexible than Liberty Mutual staff adjusters. It is important to confirm whether your policy is through Liberty Mutual or Safeco, as coverage details differ.

### How Your Claim Works
1. CRC confirms whether your policy is Liberty Mutual or Safeco
2. An adjuster (staff or independent) inspects your property
3. CRC submits a complete scope using current pricing
4. Supplements follow standard industry process

### What CRC Does for You
- CRC verifies your exact policy and coverage details before work begins
- If you have Safeco's "enhanced dwelling replacement" endorsement, CRC uses GRC-equivalent leverage
- Independent adjusters receive our complete documentation package

### Timeline
Initial inspection: 1-2 weeks
First estimate: 1-2 weeks after inspection
Supplement response: 2-3 weeks

### Key Things to Know
- Safeco independent adjusters are generally more flexible than Liberty Mutual staff
- Check if you have enhanced dwelling replacement coverage -- this is equivalent to guaranteed replacement cost
- CRC handles all coordination between you and the adjusting team
