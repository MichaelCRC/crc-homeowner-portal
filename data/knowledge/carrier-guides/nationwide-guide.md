# CRC Homeowner Guide -- Nationwide

## NATIONWIDE

### What to Expect
Nationwide is headquartered in Columbus, which means their adjusters know local conditions well. They use a digital portal for claims management and supplement submissions, making the process transparent and traceable.

### How Your Claim Works
1. Nationwide assigns a local adjuster
2. Inspection and estimate are processed through their digital portal
3. CRC submits supplements through the same portal with full documentation
4. Nationwide responds within their standard processing window

### What CRC Does for You
- CRC submits all documentation through Nationwide's portal for full traceability
- We follow up within 5 business days of every submission
- All communication is documented in writing

### Timeline
Initial inspection: 1-2 weeks
First estimate: 1 week after inspection
Supplement response: 1-2 weeks via portal

### Key Things to Know
- Everything is tracked in the portal -- you can request access to view your claim status
- Nationwide's Columbus-based adjusters have strong local knowledge
- CRC leverages the digital portal for efficient supplement processing
