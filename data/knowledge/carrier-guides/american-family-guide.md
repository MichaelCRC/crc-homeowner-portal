# CRC Homeowner Guide -- American Family

## AMERICAN FAMILY

### What to Expect
American Family has above-average claims satisfaction and their adjusters are generally accessible and responsive. They have a dedicated text intake path for storm claims, which means they process high volumes efficiently.

### How Your Claim Works
1. File your claim (text STORM to AMFAM for fastest intake)
2. American Family assigns an adjuster
3. CRC presents a complete HAAG-formatted inspection report
4. Adjusters respond well to thorough, organized documentation

### What CRC Does for You
- CRC prepares a HAAG-standard documentation package at initial inspection
- Complete upfront packages get the best results with American Family
- Supplements are submitted with full IRC code citations

### Timeline
Initial inspection: 1-2 weeks
First estimate: 1 week after inspection
Supplement response: 1-2 weeks

### Key Things to Know
- American Family responds well to professionalism and complete documentation
- CRC's HAAG inspection package is designed to match what AmFam adjusters expect
- Their adjusters are accessible -- CRC communicates directly on your behalf
