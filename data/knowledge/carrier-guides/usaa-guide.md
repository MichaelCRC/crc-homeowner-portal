# CRC Homeowner Guide -- USAA

## USAA

### What to Expect
USAA has the highest claims satisfaction in the industry. Their policies typically include RCV coverage, and their adjusters respond well to professional, thorough documentation. USAA often uses Alacrity Solutions as their independent adjusting firm.

### How Your Claim Works
1. USAA assigns an adjuster (often through Alacrity Solutions)
2. The adjuster inspects your property
3. CRC presents a complete HAAG inspection package
4. USAA processes the claim based on documented damage

### What CRC Does for You
- CRC prepares a full HAAG documentation package at initial inspection
- Alacrity adjusters respond well to organized, complete packages
- Our goal is first-pass approval with no supplement needed

### Timeline
Initial inspection: 1-2 weeks
First estimate: within 1 week of inspection
Supplement response: 1-2 weeks (typically fast)

### Key Things to Know
- USAA has the highest satisfaction rating -- they generally process claims fairly
- Complete documentation at the first inspection produces the best outcome
- RCV coverage is standard -- your full repair cost is covered minus deductible
- Alacrity adjusters are typically more flexible than staff adjusters at other carriers
